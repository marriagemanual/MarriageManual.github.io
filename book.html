The Compass

Answer honestly. Reveal together.

The bit that makes it work: no peeking, no performing. You each answer privately, then turn the page at the same time.

  1. 1

    Answer privately

    Each partner works through the night's prompts on their own side — honestly, without watching for the other's reaction.

  2. 2

    The simultaneous reveal

    You share at the same moment. No one edits their answer to match. That's where the real conversation starts.

  3. 3

    Set two dials together

    Every date closes on the Compass — Strength (how solid this area feels) and Sync (how similarly you each see it). Watch your shape fill in over ten nights.
